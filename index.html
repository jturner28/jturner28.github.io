About Me

Hi, I'm Jacob Turner and I'm a PhD candidate in astrophysics at West Virginia University. I use radio emission from pulsars to probe the structure and behavior of the interstellar medium. I'm also exploring applications of cyclic spectroscopy to pulsar timing.
