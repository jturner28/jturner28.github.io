About Me

Hi, I'm Jacob Turner, and I'm a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Green Bank Observatory. I use radio emission from pulsars to probe the structure and behavior of the interstellar medium, as well as aid the NANOGrav collaboration in the detection of nanohertz frequency gravitational waves. I'm currenlty exploring applications of cyclic spectroscopy to pulsar timing, and helping to develop the world's first cyclic spectroscopy backend for the GBT.
